What was implemented
Below items are available only as an admin.

1. Edit runway names in a plan.

There are two ways to do this.  One located here:  runway/manage.  And another directly from the plan itself.
Departments > Manage Departments > Select Department “View All Plans” > Edit/View Plan > Edit

2. Edit stage names in a plan.
There are two ways to do this.  One located here:  stage/manage.  And another directly from the plan itself.
Departments > Manage Departments > Select Department “View All Plans” > Edit/View Plan > Edit

3. Enter the text of checkpoints using CKEditor.
Can be seen in both add and edit checkpoints.
